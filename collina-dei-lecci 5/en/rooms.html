    "Il Sasso" Apartment

    6 guests · 3 double rooms · Ground floor · Exclusive rental of the whole apartment

    Il Sasso is on the ground floor of the farmhouse. It opens onto the large lawn through a wide arched glass door — the lawn is equipped with a table, chairs, barbecue and loungers, and there is a hammock in the adjoining grove. The apartment is rented only as a whole: three double rooms with TV (one with private bathroom, two with shared bathroom), a living room with sofa, a large dining table and fireplace, and a fully equipped kitchen.

    • 3 double rooms with TV (1 private bathroom · 1 shared bathroom with shower)
    • Living room with sofa, dining table and fireplace
    • Equipped kitchen — fridge, dishwasher, coffee machine, full crockery
    • Lawn with table, chairs, barbecue and loungers · hammock in the grove
    • Bed, bath and kitchen linen included · hairdryer
    • Washing machine available
    • Pets allowed on prior notice (state number, breed and size)
    • Whole apartment rental only

    "Lo Spigo" Apartment

    3 double rooms · Upper floor · Whole or by single room

    Lo Spigo occupies the upper floor of the farmhouse. The rooms have been restored in the Tuscan tradition, with exposed beams. Each room has a private bathroom with shower and a TV; access is through the shared living-kitchen. Each room can enjoy the outdoor space with table and barbecue. It can be booked as a whole or by single room.

    • Exposed beams
    • Each room: private bathroom with shower and TV
    • Access through the shared living-kitchen
    • Outdoor space with table and barbecue
    • Bed, bath and kitchen linen included · hairdryer
    • Washing machine available
    • Pets allowed on prior notice (state number, breed and size)
    • Bookable as a whole or by single room
